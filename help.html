üîê Account & Sign Up

How do I create an account?

  • Click "Sign Up / Login" in the navigation
  • Switch to the "Register" tab
  • Choose a unique username (3-20 characters, letters, numbers, and underscores)
  • Pick a display name (how others will see you)
  • Enter your email and create a password (minimum 6 characters)
  • Check your email for a verification link
  • Click the link to verify and you're all set!

What's the difference between username and display name?

Your @username is unique and used in your profile URL (e.g., lillyko.space/yourname). Your display name is what people see and can have spaces and special characters.

üë§ Profile Customization

How do I change my profile picture?

  • Go to your profile page
  • Click on your profile picture (you'll see a camera icon when hovering)
  • Select an image from your device
  • Crop and adjust as needed (images are automatically cropped to square)
  • Click "Upload" to save your new profile picture

How do I edit my bio?

  • Go to your profile page
  • Click the "Edit" button next to your bio section
  • Type your new bio (up to 500 characters)
  • Click "Save" to update your bio

Profile Picture Requirements:

  • Square images work best (1:1 ratio)
  • Maximum file size: 5MB
  • Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF
  • Images are automatically cropped to 500x500 pixels

üë§ Profile

How do I view my profile?

Click your name in the top right corner and select "Profile" from the dropdown menu.

Can others see my profile?

Yes! Anyone can visit your profile at lillyko.space/yourusername

What is my User ID number?

Your User ID is a sequential number showing when you joined. The first person to sign up gets #1, the second gets #2, and so on!

üîë Login Issues

I can't log in. What should I do?

  • Make sure you've verified your email address
  • Check that you're using the correct password
  • If you forgot your password, you'll need to contact support (password reset coming soon)
  • Clear your browser cache and try again

I didn't receive a verification email

Check your spam folder. If it's not there, log in and click "Resend verification email" on the message that appears.

üö™ Logging Out

To log out, click your name in the top right corner and select "Log Out" from the dropdown menu.
